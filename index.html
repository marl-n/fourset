Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tar surfacing, paving, and construction services.

How much does tar surfacing cost in Gauteng?

The cost of tar surfacing in Gauteng typically ranges between R110 – R250 per square meter, depending on the size of the project, site conditions, and materials used. Contact us for a free, accurate quote.

Do you offer residential driveway paving?

Yes, we provide professional driveway paving and resurfacing services throughout Gauteng and surroundings. Whether it’s a new installation or repairs, we ensure a durable and visually appealing finish.

Which areas in Gauteng do you serve?

We cover all major towns and cities in Gauteng, including Pretoria, Centurion, Kempton Park, Midrand, Sandton, Benoni, Vereeniging, Potchefstroom and other areas surrounding Gauteng.

How long does a tar surfacing project take?

Most residential projects such as driveways can be completed in 1–3 days, while larger projects like roads or parking lots may take longer depending on size and weather conditions.

Do you also construct sports courts?

Yes, we specialize in tennis, basketball, netball, and multi-sport courts. We use high-quality materials to ensure a safe, durable playing surface for schools, communities, and private clients.
