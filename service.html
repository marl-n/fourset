Got Questions? We've Got The Answers.

The cost of tar driveway installation varies based on factors such as size, location, and site conditions. On average, prices range from R120 to R150 per square metre. For a precise quote, please contact us with your project details.

Asphalt surfacing is ideal for driveways, parking lots, roads, and pathways due to its durability, smooth finish, and cost-effectiveness. It provides a strong surface that can withstand heavy traffic and various weather conditions.

Tar is a natural product derived from coal, while asphalt is a petroleum-based product. Tar is more flexible and resistant to water damage, making it suitable for colder climates. Asphalt is more commonly used due to its lower cost and ease of application.

The cost of tar driveway installation varies based on factors such as size, location, and site conditions. On average, prices range from R120 to R150 per square metre. For a precise quote, please contact us with your project details.

The installation of a tar driveway typically takes 1 to 5 days, depending on the size and complexity of the project. This includes site preparation, laying the tar, and allowing it to cure properly

Asphalt surfacing can last between 15 to 20 years with proper maintenance. Regular sealing and repairs can extend its lifespan and keep it looking good.

Yes, there are different types of tar driveways, including traditional tar and chip, tar and gravel, and tar macadam. Each type offers different aesthetics and performance characteristics.
